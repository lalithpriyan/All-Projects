Project: Power BI | Regional Sales Overview Dashboard

Summary:
This project involved developing a Power BI-based regional sales overview dashboard covering the period from 01-01-2024 to 30-12-2024. The dashboard provides comprehensive insights into sales performance across different U.S. regions, with year-over-year comparisons and state-wise analysis to support strategic decision-making.

Client Requirements:
The client required a Sales Overview Dashboard with the following objectives:
1. Regional Performance: Track sales across Central, East, South, and West regions.
2. Temporal Analysis: Monitor monthly sales trends for each region.
3. Geographic Visualization: Display sales distribution across states.
4. Comparative Metrics: Show Year-over-Year (YoY) performance.
5. Multi-metric Analysis: Track Sales, Profit, and Quantity.
6. State-level Insights: Analyze performance by individual states.
7. Historical Comparison: Compare current year with previous year metrics.
8. Filter Functionality: Enable year-based and measure-based filtering.

Insights and Final Outcome:
