Project: Excel | E-commerce Sales Overview Dashboard

Summary:
This project involved designing an Excel-based interactive dashboard to analyze sales performance for an e-commerce business. The dashboard provides detailed insights into sales trends, customer segmentation, geographic distribution, and product category performance. It enables stakeholders to identify key drivers of profitability and make data-driven decisions to optimize operations and strategy.

Client Requirements:
The client required an E-commerce Sales Overview Dashboard with the following objectives:
1. Total Sales: Display overall sales revenue for the selected period.
2. Total Profit: Calculate and display total profit with associated profit margin percentages.
3. Total Order Quantity: Summarize the number of orders processed.
4. Monthly Sales Trends: Visualize sales trends across months to understand seasonality.
5. Market Region Insights: Showcase sales distribution by geographic regions using a pie chart.
6. Customer Segments: Analyze sales based on customer types (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office).
7. Shipping Modes: Highlight orders processed under different shipping methods.
8. Top & Bottom Performers: List top 5 and bottom 5 categories and countries by sales revenue.

Stakeholders:
1. E-commerce Business Owners
2. Sales and Marketing Teams
3. Supply Chain Managers
4. Financial Analysts
5. Customer Service Teams
6. Product Development Teams
7. Logistics and Operations Teams
8. Investors and Business Partners

Steps in Project:

Insights and Final Outcome:
1. Total sales revenue recorded was $2,64,74,659, with a total profit of $24,95,874 and an average profit margin of 10.86%.
2. The total order quantity was 2,84,209, indicating a significant volume of transactions.
3. Monthly sales trends showed consistent revenue throughout the year, with slight peaks in certain months.
4. Market regions such as Europe (25%) and LATAM (24%) dominated the sales distribution.
5. The Consumer segment ($13.85M) accounted for the largest share of sales compared to Corporate and Home Office.
6. Cardio Equipment ($4.66M) led the top categories, while Baby ($11.40K) ranked as the lowest-performing category.
7. The United States ($3.71M) emerged as the top-performing country, while India ($0.59M) contributed the least among the top 10.
8. The dashboard provided actionable insights to optimize product performance and regional sales strategies.
