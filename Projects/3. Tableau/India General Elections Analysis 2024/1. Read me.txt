Project: Excel | India General Elections Analysis 2024

Summary:
This project involved creating a Tableau-based election results dashboard for India's 2024 General Election, focusing on state and constituency-level analysis. The dashboard provides comprehensive insights into voting patterns, party performance, and candidate statistics for informed electoral analysis.

Client Requirements:
The client required an Election Results Dashboard with the following objectives:
1. Election Overview: Display key electoral statistics and results
2. Constituency Analysis: Show detailed constituency-wise results
3. Party Performance: Track seats won by different parties and alliances
4. Vote Distribution: Analyze total votes, EVM votes, and postal votes
5. Candidate Analysis: Display top performing candidates
6. Party Alliance Tracking: Monitor performance of major political alliances
7. Vote Share Analysis: Calculate percentage vote shares
8. Filtering Capabilities: Enable detailed constituency and state-level analysis

Stakeholders:
1. Election Commission Officials
2. Political Parties
3. Constituency Representatives
4. Media Organizations
5. Political Analysts
6. Election Observers
7. Government Officials
8. General Public

Insights and Final Outcome:
1. Electoral Scale: 82 total seats with 842 candidates across 6 parties
2. Voter Participation: 8,99,73,014 total votes cast (8,97,09,566 EVM, 2,63,448 postal)
3. Party Performance: SP leads with 37 seats, followed by BJP with 35 seats
4. Alliance Distribution: I.N.D.I.A. Alliance won 43 seats, NDA won 38 seats
5. Vote Share: BJP leads with 42.32% (3,75,00,612 votes)
6. Top Candidate: Raj Nath Singh (BJP) won Lucknow with 53.89% votes
7. Margin Analysis: Winning margin of 1,35,159 votes in highlighted constituency
